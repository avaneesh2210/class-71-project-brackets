library



was built in 1960

The library in our school has many books. We have a library period every week and we get an opportunity to take one book every week. Our English teacher suggests us some books which we can read. I like to read the Enid Blyton's books from there.

auditorium

was built in 1980

The auditorium in our school is very big. It has a capacity for holding about 500 people. We have our annual day function here. We also visit there on special days like Children's Day, Teacher's Day and Independence Day. I participated in the drama skit wherein I got an opportunity to be on the stage of this auditorium.



church

The church is where we go for prayers in the week. Every morning, the bell is rung which I like very much. There is a statue of baby Jesus Christ who is being held by Mother Mary inside the chruch. There are many chairs for people to pray and when we go there the father of the church tells us some stories from the Bible to us.
